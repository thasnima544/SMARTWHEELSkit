About SmartWheels

SmartWheels is an AI-powered wheelchair designed to provide enhanced mobility and safety for individuals with disabilities. It features obstacle detection, voice commands, and emergency response systems to ensure smooth and secure movement.

About our Innovation

Real-time obstacle detection using AI and sensors

Voice command integration for hands-free control

GPS tracking for location monitoring

Automatic alerts sent to emergency contacts

How it works ??

Sensors placed on the wheelchair detect obstacles and respond to voice commands. If an emergency occurs, the system halts movement and sends an alert with the user's location to designated contacts, ensuring swift assistance.
